About @Tend

Founded by five Santa Clara University students inspired to help dealing with Multiple Sclerosis.
Research shows that one of the key needs of an individual affected by MS is a strong support system and excellent care. The purpose of @Tend is to cater to these needs by providing the individuals and their families an easy way to find qualified caregivers, who have experience in dealing with the varying progressive states and associated complications of the ailment. The website provides a comprehensive network of caregivers which allows the users and families to search and request for caregivers based on a specific set of criteria.
